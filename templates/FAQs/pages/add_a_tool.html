Then, click the big green button in the center bottom that says "+ Add a Tool".
From there, fill in the details and optionally add an image of the tool. The Features section is visible to anyone who can see your tool. The private notes will only be visible to you. When complete, press the "Add Tool" button. Now you've got a new tool in your toolbox.
