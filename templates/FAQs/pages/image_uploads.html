At this point, there is only capability to upload one image for each tool. Once it is uploaded, you can always change it - either immediately or you can change it later while editing a tool.

Your images are stored in an encrypted AWS storage location and cannot be accessed from outside of this app. Any member who has visibility of the tool can view the image and potentially screenshot or download the image at that point.
