Cross Platform Support

Use on any device

As a web app, you can access and manage your tools on any device. Upload photos of your tools directly from your smartphone.

It's even possible to install the web app on your mobile device to make it feel more like a native app.

Features

Find tools you need

Search for the tools that you'll need in your next project. You'll see all of the tools that are being shared in all of your neighborhoods, and can filter by type (hand, power, garden, fastening, or other). If you can't find what you need just Ask a Neighbor. People will have tools that they haven't loaded in the app yet.

Manage your tool inventory

Easily store images of your tools, track their health, and store notes about the tools. You can have public notes that share the features/usage/care instructions/fees for others to see who may be interested in using your tool. There are also private notes where you can save other details like purchase date, cost, and who should or shouldn't borrow this tool. Also control which tools get shared in which neighborhoods.

Interract with your community

Approve or reject peoples requests to borrow your tools. View notifications for actions, messages, and other items.

Switch between light and dark mode in your account settings.

About the Creator

I originally made this web app as a personal project to develop my programming skills. It started as my capstone project to the amazing (and free) CS50 course, but then I continued to add more and more features. I learned how to publish and host it, and realized that people could actually use this.
Ultimately, I do want people to reconsider every purchase they make. If more poeple share tools, we will all help reduce the total amount of waste we continue to generate.
